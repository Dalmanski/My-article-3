A Snack to Remember: Enjoying Ngohiong at the CTU Canteen

Let's explore my experience of buying Ngohiong at the CTU Canteen.

A Delicious Ngohiong Experience
A Boy Watching Ngohiong in the Canteen
The boy stands at the canteen, looking at the tasty Ngohiong on display. Ngohiong is a popular fried food filled with vegetables and sometimes meat. He seems curious and excited about trying it. The crispy look of the Ngohiong grabs his attention immediately.
Buying the Ngohiong
The boy decides to buy the Ngohiong because it looks delicious. He carefully picks one from the tray and pays the vendor. The golden-brown color makes it very tempting. Holding it in his hands, he feels eager to take the first bite.
Eating Ngohiong While Standing
He starts eating the Ngohiong right away, savoring its crispy and flavorful taste. Standing in the canteen, he enjoys each bite with a smile. It’s warm and satisfying, perfect for a quick snack. He finishes it quickly because it’s so tasty.
Throwing the Trash in the Bin
After finishing the Ngohiong, he looks for a trashcan to dispose of the wrapper. He responsibly throws it into the bin to keep the place clean. Taking care of trash shows he values cleanliness. Feeling full and happy, he walks away, ready for the rest of his day.

About CTU Canteen

The CTU Canteen provides students, faculty, and staff with affordable and delicious meals throughout the day. Located at the heart of the campus, it serves a variety of nutritious dishes designed to fuel productivity and promote a healthy lifestyle. Whether you’re in the mood for a quick snack or a full meal, the CTU Canteen is the place to go for a satisfying dining experience.
